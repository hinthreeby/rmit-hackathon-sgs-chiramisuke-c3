1. Objective
- Generate 2D visual and audio assets for the Climate Change Card Game.
The assets should support an educational and eco-friendly aesthetic, representing real-world environmental actions and concepts.
2. Visual Style
- 2D, flat, and friendly cartoon style.
- Color palette: Symbolizing nature and balance.
- Rounded corners, smooth gradients, minimal shadows.
- Consistent line thickness and visual simplicity.
